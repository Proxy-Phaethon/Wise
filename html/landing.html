Frequently Asked Questions

What is WISE?

WISE is a personal and professional productivity system designed to help individuals and teams manage tasks, requests, scheduling, and documentation—all in one place.

Who is WISE for?

Anyone who wants a streamlined workspace! Whether you're a team manager, freelancer, student, or just someone who loves organizing their life, WISE has tools for you.

What features does WISE offer?

WISE includes a dashboard, request forms, document editor, calendar, task tracking, daily updates, and even a cute awards system. More features are on the way!

Is WISE free to use?

WISE will have both free and premium versions. The free version covers all essential tools, while premium plans unlock advanced features for power users.

How is WISE different from other productivity tools?

Unlike rigid corporate tools, WISE is customizable, user-friendly, and has personality. It's built with retro aesthetics, gamification elements, and Bastet’s signature touch.
